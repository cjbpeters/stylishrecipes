Watermelon Haloumi Salad

Ingredients

  • Small to medium sized seedless watermelon
  • 2 blocks haloumi
  • 2 jalapeños
  • 1/2 bag arugula
  • 40 g pickled red onion
  • orange-lime vinegrette:
    • 2 limes
    • orange
    • 30 g redwine vinegar
    • 10 g mustard
    • 8 g honey
    • 5 g salt
    • 3 g pepper
    • 50 g evoo
    • 75 g blended oil

Instructions

  1. Cut watermelon into slices, around 1 1/2 to 2 inches thick. Salt lightly and set over cooling rack to drain slightly
  2. Zest and juice your citrus.
  3. Make your vinegrette by combining your citrus juice and all other non oil ingredients in a blender. Blend thoroughly, then gradually add oil until uniformly emulsified.
  4. Seed and fine dice jalapeños.
  5. Cut haloumi into 1 inch cubes.
  6. Grill watermelon slices over a gas or charcoal grill until it has good looking grill marks, deepened color and has dehydrated slightly.
  7. Cut watermelon off the rind and into medium-large cubes
  8. Toss all ingredients but the cheese with your vinegrette and plate.
  9. Using a nuetral oil, pan or deep-fry haloumi cubes until golden brown.
  10. Distribute cheese cubes evenly over salads, serve immediately.
