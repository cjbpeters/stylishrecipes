Elvis tarts

Peanut butter, banana and caramel tarts inspired by the famous sandwich of choice of our greatest tv murderer.

Components

  • Tartlet shells
  • Bruleed bananas
  • Cardamom pepper infused dark rum
  • Whipped cream
  • Peanut butter caramel sauce
  • Bacon peanut brittle

Process

  1. Prepare tartlets and infused rum.
  2. Flambe 6ozs of infused rum in a saucepan, reduce by 1/4 to 1/3, add 1 oz of dark maple syrup and cool. Seperate in 1/2.
  3. Prepare brittle.
  4. Prepare caramel sauce.
  5. Fill tartlet shells with 1/2 inch layer of caramel sauce. Brulee bananas, add layer on top of caramel sauce, cover in rum reduction.
  6. Cover bananas in caramel sauce until just shy of the top of your tartlet shells.
  7. Make whipped cream, adding a pinch of salt, scoop of sugar and other 1/2 of rum reduction.
  8. Top tartlets with whipped cream, stick good-sized piece of brittle on top.
